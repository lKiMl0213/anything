# Feature: Crafting And Gathering

## What
Crafting and gathering let the player harvest resources, improve profession skills, and turn materials into useful outputs such as equipment, consumables, or components.

## Why
This feature adds alternative progression outside direct combat. It creates longer-term resource planning, supports the item loop, and gives value to exploration and collected materials.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] The player can gather resources from valid gathering nodes
- [ ] The player can craft recipes they meet requirements for
- [ ] Profession progress improves access to more advanced content
- [ ] Gathered and crafted outputs enter the normal inventory and item loop
- [ ] Crafting and gathering can contribute to broader progression goals
