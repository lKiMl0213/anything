# Feature: Items, Loot, And Economy

## What
The item and economy systems let players collect loot, equip gear, manage inventory space, use class-compatible equipment, buy and sell goods, and benefit from rarities and randomized rewards.

## Why
This feature makes victories and progression feel rewarding. It turns exploration and combat into tangible advancement through better gear, more resources, and stronger economic choices.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Monsters and activities can reward items and currencies
- [ ] The player can equip valid gear and manage inventory capacity
- [ ] Loot can vary in rarity and quality
- [ ] Shops can exchange items for gold or cash currencies where applicable
- [ ] Equipment compatibility respects class progression lines
- [ ] Archer ammunition can be managed through the quiver flow
